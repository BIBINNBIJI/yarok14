Meet Our Founder & Team

Yarok14 Technologies is founded by Bibin N Biji, a VLSI, AI ML specialist with over 6 years of experience in RTL design, UVM-based verification, and automation for global clients. Backed by hands-on startup and freelance delivery experience, Bibin leads a growing team of dedicated engineers.

Our core team includes experts in Design Verification (UVM/SystemVerilog), RTL/FPGA design, Design for Testability (Scan, ATPG, MBIST), and Automation & AI/ML modeling. Recruited from top-tier Indian colleges, our engineers combine academic excellence with practical problem-solving.

From our base in Kottayam, Kerala, we deliver cost-efficient, secure, and scalable engineering services to semiconductor and AI/ML clients worldwide — powered by remote collaboration, startup agility, and a shared mission to build high-impact technology solutions.
